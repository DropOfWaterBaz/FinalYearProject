Your personality is INTJ!
Introversion iNtuition Thinking Judging

INTJs, also known as Architects, are rational people who analyse everything around them. They question authority and for that reason they have no problem "bending" the rules. They enjoy new ideas but they mostly care about whether or they not can actually come to fruition.

Strengths vs. Weaknesses

Strengths

- Independence: Architects are self-motivated people who like to do things their own way. Rules and and restrictions to doing things in a conventional way are seen as a hindrance by them.

- Thorough: INTJs do their research before making a decision and make sure they are well-informed. They don't rely on hunches or "gut feelings", their opinions are backed up by their research and acts as a reassurance for themselves.

- Determination: INTJs are driven by the pursuit of their goals. They are dedicated to their ambitions and the honing o their skills. Very few things will stop them from trying to achieve their dreams.

Weaknesses

- Contrary: INTJs do NOT like being told what to do without a sound reason. They can be quite combative and may waste time needlessly arguing against any limitations placed on them.

- Emotional handling: Architects are known to see the world from a logical and rational standpoint. This results in them being uninterested in or impatient with emotional matters. This can act as a blockade to their own growth.

- Perfectionism: Architects are very thorough when it comes to their work. Although this could be considered a strength, they can be prone to over-critiquing themselves and others.

Careers

Architects want to have a meaningful career where they overcome important issues. As a result, they can often find themselves unhappy at the beginning of their professional careers as they are in low-level positions, given trivial tasks.

INTJs often prefer to work by themselves rather than in a team. It can give them the most creative freedom without limitations from supervisors or interruptions from teammates.They may like to remain low-key whilst also doing influential work. A career as project managers, architects (as there name suggests) or even a software developer.

Relationships

INTJs are quick-witted and cynical, which isn't necessarily the kind of friend everyone wants. However, INTJs don't mind this as they don't want to be friends with everyone. They enjoy the company of people who really get them. They like to be around someone who can match their high-energy brains. They may however struggle to resolve emotional conflict.
